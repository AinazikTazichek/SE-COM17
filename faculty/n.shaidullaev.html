Biography

Holds a bachelor's and master's degrees from International Ataturk Alatoo University.

Before coming to IAAU, N. Shaidullaev worked as Informatics teacher, Republic olympiad and IOI (International Olympiad in Informatics) trainer in Kyrgyz-Turkish Boys High-School named after Ch. Aytmatov. He is one of two founding directors of Research and Development Center of IAAU.

Publications
