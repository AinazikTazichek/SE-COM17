Materials to be submitted for Defense:


Title

Title Page must be prepared in 4 languages: English, Kyrgyz, Russian, Turkish
Abstract





 Abstract Page must also be prepared in 4 languages : English,Kyrgyz,Russian,Turkish
An abstract is a synopsis of the contents of a thesis. It must be concise. It must not exceed 50 words. It should contain only essential information about your project. In 50 words or less, it describes the objective (thesis statement), methods, and conclusions of your paper. The wording is direct, with no information repeated. It does not include background information, descriptions of methods, literature reviews, or references to other works.


References










 References to the sources must be prepared in APA Style.
Basic Essentials of a Reference
- Author(s) or Editor(s)
- Date
- Title
- Place
- Publisher

Introduction


 In 250 words or less, it describes the objective (thesis statement), methods, and conclusions of your paper. The wording is direct, with no information repeated. It does not include background information, descriptions of methods, literature reviews, or references to other works.
